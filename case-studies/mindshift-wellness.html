    MindShift Plus Wellness

    A calming digital presence for a faith-integrated mental health clinic — values-aligned care, professionally presented online.

    Client: MindShift Plus Wellness Services: Web Design, UX Live at: mindshiftwellnessclinic.org

    The Challenge

    MindShift Plus Wellness serves people at vulnerable moments — seeking help for mental health while integrating faith into their healing journey. Their website needed to feel safe, professional, and approachable — not clinical or overwhelming.

    Visitors needed to quickly understand what services are offered, who the team is, and how to take the first step toward care.

    Our Approach

    We prioritized emotional design: soft color palettes, generous whitespace, clear typography, and language that speaks with compassion rather than jargon.

    • Service pages structured for clarity and trust
    • Prominent contact and intake pathways
    • Accessible, readable layout on all devices
    • Brand visuals that reflect holistic wellness
    Before
    Limited online presence that didn't convey the clinic's warmth or faith-integrated approach. Potential clients couldn't easily understand services or how to reach out.
    After

    What Changed After Launch

    Visitors now understand the clinic's services and intake process on their first visit — without the clinical coldness that often drives people away from seeking help. The site communicates faith-integrated care clearly, so potential clients know they're entering a space that honors both professional mental health practice and spiritual conviction.
