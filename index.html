Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Jerless

"Faith deserves world-class creativity."

As a designer, developer, and lifelong believer, I saw a gap between the quality of mainstream media and what was available for churches and ministries. I set out to close that gap — building brands, apps, and experiences that honor God and serve His people.

Today, BFL Studios is home to six brands and growing. Every project we take on is personal — because this isn't just work. It's ministry through media.
