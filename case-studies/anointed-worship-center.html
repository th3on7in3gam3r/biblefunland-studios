    Anointed Worship Center

    A welcoming digital front door for a growing ministry community.

    Client: Anointed Worship Center Services: Web Design, Branding Live at: anointedworshipcenter.com

    The Challenge

    AWC needed a public-facing website that answered the questions every first-time visitor has: Who are you? When do you meet? How do I get involved? Their previous online presence was outdated and didn't reflect the energy and heart of the congregation.

    The site also needed to serve existing members — pointing them to events, ministries, and the AWC Vault member platform without overwhelming newcomers.

    Our Approach

    We designed around two user journeys: the curious visitor and the connected member. Clear calls-to-action, service information above the fold, and ministry pages that tell stories rather than list bullet points.

    • Visitor-first homepage with clear next steps
    • Ministry and events sections built for discovery
    • Mobile-responsive layout for on-the-go access
    • Visual identity aligned with AWC's worship culture
    Before
    Outdated site that didn't reflect the church's vision. Visitors struggled to find service times and connection pathways. No cohesive visual identity online.
    After

    What Changed After Launch

    First-time visitors can find service times, ministries, and connection pathways without emailing or calling the office. The site reflects AWC's worship culture visually — and links members to AWC Vault for deeper resources. The church now has a professional digital presence that matches the quality of their in-person ministry.
