    AWC Vault

    A secure members-only hub for sermons, media, and ministry resources.

    Client: Anointed Worship Center Services: Web & Digital, UX Strategy Live at: AWC Vault

    The Challenge

    Anointed Worship Center's teaching content, media files, and member resources lived across Google Drive folders, WhatsApp groups, and email threads. New members didn't know where to look. Leaders spent time re-sharing the same links. There was no single, secure place for the congregation to access everything the church had built.

    They needed a private platform — not a public website page — that felt professional, was easy to navigate on mobile, and could grow as their media library expanded.

    Our Approach

    We started with a discovery session mapping every resource type AWC wanted to host: sermon archives, event media, study materials, and announcements. From there we designed an information architecture around how members actually search — by series, topic, and date — not how files were stored internally.

    • Secure member authentication and role-based access
    • Mobile-first layout for on-the-go listening and reading
    • Clean categorization for sermons, resources, and media
    • Scalable structure for future content types
